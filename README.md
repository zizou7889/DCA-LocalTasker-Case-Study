# DCA LocalTasker Case Study

## Digital Growth Strategy for Lead Generation and Conversion

---

## Project Overview

This project presents a **digital customer acquisition (DCA) strategy** for LocalTasker, focused on strengthening its online presence, increasing qualified leads, and improving customer conversion and retention.

The case study evaluates LocalTasker’s target market, identifies key customer pain points, and proposes data-backed digital, partnership, and platform enhancements to drive sustainable growth.

---

## Business Context

LocalTasker operates in the on-demand services marketplace, connecting customers with local professionals for installation, maintenance, and renovation-related tasks.

The business challenge addressed in this project is:

* Increasing brand visibility among high-intent customers
* Building trust in service quality and reliability
* Improving conversion from discovery to booking
* Enhancing customer retention in a competitive market

---

## Target Audience

The primary target segment includes:

* Young homeowners and first-time renovators
* Age group: 25–40 years
* Time-poor and cost-conscious consumers
* Millennials interested in smart homes and appliances
* Australians planning minor home renovations within the next year

---

## Customer Pain Points

The strategy is designed around three core customer concerns:

* **Cost** – Fear of hidden charges and price uncertainty
* **Quality** – Difficulty assessing contractor capability
* **Reliability** – Trust issues related to service delivery and timelines

Addressing these pain points is central to improving conversion and long-term loyalty.

---

## Digital Acquisition Strategy

### Targeted Advertising

* Paid social campaigns on Facebook and Instagram
* Search-based targeting for high-intent queries such as:

  * “Painting supply store near me”
  * “How much does it cost to renovate my kitchen”

### Content and Messaging Focus

* Cost transparency
* Verified professionals
* Convenience and speed

---

## Partnership Strategy

Strategic partnerships are proposed to extend LocalTasker’s reach and credibility:

* Appliance stores and manufacturers
* Hardware retailers offering bundled services
* Property developers and property managers

**Example Value Proposition:**
“Buy today and receive complimentary installation from a LocalTasker expert.”

These partnerships create mutual value by increasing sales for partners while generating high-intent leads for LocalTasker.

---

## Platform Enhancements (App & Website)

To improve user experience and trust, the project recommends:

* Location-based contractor filtering
* AI chatbot for basic queries and task guidance
* Verified contractor and customer profiles
* Ratings, reviews, and customer testimonials
* Contractor portfolios showcasing previous work
* Price transparency and upfront estimates
* One-click booking and real-time task updates
* Auto-translation features for accessibility

These features directly address reliability and quality concerns while reducing booking friction.

---

## Conversion Funnel Design

The proposed funnel follows a structured customer journey:

### Awareness

* Targeted ads
* Social media engagement
* Strategic partnerships

### Consideration

* Website and app browsing
* Reviews and ratings
* Contractor profiles and estimates

### Decision

* “Book Now” functionality
* Fast checkout and secure payment

### Action

* Task completion
* Status updates and notifications

### Retention

* Reviews and feedback
* Loyalty programs
* Rebooking reminders

---

The proposed strategy delivers value by:

* Increasing qualified lead volume
* Improving conversion rates
* Strengthening brand trust and credibility
* Reducing customer acquisition friction
* Supporting long-term customer retention
